A message from our founder, and the philosophy that guides how we work.

Message from the CEO

Built on purpose,
guided by people.

Having Corp. was founded with a single, clear purpose: to become a trusted specialist in the sourcing and supply of cosmetic ingredients.

We began by helping Korean ingredient manufacturers reach markets beyond Korea, and we have built a growing export track record ever since. Our intent is to remain a dependable bridge for the K-beauty ingredient companies driving this industry forward, supporting their expansion onto the global stage.

At the same time, we supply the innovative active ingredients that Korean brands and OEM / ODM partners need to realize their own global ambitions. By bringing these actives to the companies at the heart of K-beauty, we aim to play a meaningful part in its continued growth around the world.

Through the sourcing and supply of innovative actives, we will stay ahead of global trends, and we aspire to be a company that beautifies not only the skin, but also the heart, growing together with people and giving back to the society we are part of.

Our Philosophy

People are
everything.

Build the future together with Having, and you will find more than a partner, you will find the best of teams and a future worth looking forward to.

Even in the age of AI, we believe the work that truly matters is, in the end, done by people. Tools and technology will keep advancing, but judgment, trust, and care remain human. That is why people always come first for us.

HAVING connects Korean beauty ingredients, fragrance, finished products, and OEM / ODM capabilities with global partners.
