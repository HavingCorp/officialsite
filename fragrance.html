Fragrance Solutions

Our Approach

Fragrance begins long
before the bottle.

A finished scent is the final step of a long journey, one that begins with raw materials, technical expertise, creative direction, and the right partners behind it.

HAVING brings global fragrance suppliers closer to Korean brands and manufacturers, coordinating the conversations, samples, documents, and decisions that move fragrance projects forward.

The Craft

Connecting a world of scent.

The craft of fragrance carries centuries of heritage, from the historic perfume regions of France to today's specialist scent laboratories. It is a world of rare raw materials, patient development, and technical expertise shaped by people who understand scent.

HAVING's role is to make that world more accessible to Korean partners, bridging global fragrance houses and suppliers with the brands and manufacturers who turn fragrance into market-ready products.
