Responsible Trade Statement

Sustainability, shaped
for the way
we actually work.

What sustainability means at HAVING

  • Choosing suppliers and products with stronger standards when reviewing opportunities.
  • Supporting Korean makers with fair access to overseas buyers and long-term business opportunities.
  • Keeping trade practical, documented, and transparent from inquiry to export execution.

HAVING Corp. is a sourcing and export coordination partner. We do not operate factories ourselves. Our approach to sustainability therefore focuses on how we select partners, coordinate documentation, communicate across borders, and support responsible trade relationships between Korean suppliers and overseas buyers.

How We Apply It

From partner review
to export execution.

Our approach to sustainability is not a separate department. It is embedded into how we review opportunities, verify materials, coordinate stakeholders, and keep records across each transaction.

01

Select

Review products and partners based on fit, product quality, documentation readiness, and responsible business conduct.

02

Verify

Check available specifications, certifications, safety documents, and buyer-requested supporting materials.

03

Coordinate

Align buyer requirements and supplier capabilities across price, MOQ, lead time, claims, and documentation.

04

Record

Maintain structured commercial and export records so each transaction remains clear and actionable.

Three Pillars

Responsible trade,
clearly defined.

We present sustainability in the context of our actual role: a sourcing and export coordination partner working between Korean suppliers and overseas buyers.

Environmental

Responsible Sourcing

Environmental responsibility at HAVING begins with better partner and product choices, not with manufacturing claims we cannot make.

  • Consider ingredient and packaging standards during supplier review.
  • Support buyer requests for available environmental or packaging-related materials.
  • Coordinate sourcing and shipment discussions more efficiently where feasible.

Social

Expanding Opportunity

We create access: connecting Korean brands, manufacturers, and ingredient suppliers with overseas business opportunities.

  • Support Korean makers entering new markets through one coordinated trade channel.
  • Facilitate buyer communication across distributors, retailers, clinics, and professional channels.
  • Build long-term partnerships based on execution, not one-off introductions.

Governance

Clear Standards

Our governance approach is simple: communicate clearly, document properly, and keep transactions structured.

  • Clarify responsibilities, conditions, and commercial terms early.
  • Coordinate documents such as specifications, COA, MSDS, and other requested materials.
  • Keep trade practical, traceable, and proportionate to our business model.

HAVING connects Korean beauty ingredients, fragrance, finished products, and OEM / ODM capabilities with global partners.
