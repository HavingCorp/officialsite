Privacy Policy

How Having Corp. collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Having Corp. ("Having", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you visit this website or contact us. By using this website or submitting an inquiry, you acknowledge the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it through our inquiry form, namely:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • The content of your inquiry

We also process limited technical information automatically, such as a language-preference cookie required for the website to function, and, only with your consent, aggregated analytics data (see "Cookies and Analytics" below).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to and manage your inquiry, to communicate with you regarding your request, and to maintain related business records. We do not use your information for marketing without your separate consent.

3. Retention Period

We retain inquiry-related personal information until one (1) year after your inquiry has been resolved, after which it is deleted, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law. You may request earlier deletion at any time (see "Your Rights").

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — a single cookie that remembers your language preference. This cookie is strictly necessary for the website to function and is used without requiring consent.
  • Analytics cookies — we use Google Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies are loaded only after you give consent through our cookie banner, and you may decline. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set.

You can withdraw your analytics consent at any time by clearing your cookies or contacting us. For information on how Google processes data, please refer to Google's own privacy resources.

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except: (a) where you have consented; (b) to service providers who process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards (for example, Google Analytics, only after your consent); or (c) where required by law or competent authority.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or withdraw your consent, at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at having@having.co.kr. We will respond within a reasonable period in accordance with applicable law.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Users

Our company and data are based in the Republic of Korea. If you contact us from outside Korea, you understand and agree that your information will be processed and stored in Korea. We process personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Act of Korea.

9. Children

This website is intended for business users and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the website after changes take effect constitutes acknowledgement of the revised policy.

11. Privacy Officer

We have not designated a separate privacy officer position. Privacy-related matters, including requests to access, correct, or delete personal information, are handled directly by the company's legal representative.
